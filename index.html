Chris Kennedy

My name is Christopher Kennedy and I am an Interaction Design student in Belfast.

My main passions involve designing and illustrating, especially vector based art and illustrations. I also have a love of photography. What i love most about being a designer is the process of sketching ideas on paper and taking them to life in the world of pixels and vectors.

I also love designing interfaces and creating webpages to maximise user experiences; Creating code to become a fully functioning webpage is a skill I want to develop even further.
